Why don't you get the Fajr time too?

It's essential to enter the Fajr time according to a local masjid's timetable that you trust.

Fajr time can vary due to several factors, including:

  • Geographical Location: The latitude and longitude of your location can affect the time of Fajr.
  • Altitude: Higher altitudes may experience different sunrise and sunset times.
  • Seasonal Changes: The length of days varies with seasons, which can impact Fajr times.
  • Calculation Methods: Different Islamic organizations may use different methods to calculate prayer times.

Always refer to your local masjid's timetable for the most accurate prayer times.

When would Prophet Dawud sleep?

Dawud's Night Prayer

How do you know?

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said, "The most beloved prayer to Allah is the prayer of Dawud, and the most beloved fasts to Allah are the fasts of Dawud. He used to sleep half the night, pray for one-third of it, and then sleep for one-sixth of it." - Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim
