When would Prophet Dawud sleep?

Dawud's Night Prayer

How do you know?

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said, "The most beloved prayer to Allah is the prayer of Dawud, and the most beloved fasts to Allah are the fasts of Dawud. He used to sleep half the night, pray for one-third of it, and then sleep for one-sixth of it." - Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim
